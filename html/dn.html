Introduction

Devine Nexus is an environment demo set in a dystopian future where the pursuit of scientific progress has driven humanity to the brink of extinction.

The military had captured a behemoth with unique abilities, and the scientists had used its body to develop new weapons technology. They were utilizing a highly unstable form of energy extracted from the essence of the creature. However, during the containment process, a disastrous malfunction occurred, resulting in an uncontrollable discharge of this energy. This caused massive chain reactions, resulting in destructive explosions, widespread power fluctuations, complete infrastructural breakdown across the globe, and billions of human casualties.

This was a university project where I utilized my models and textures to create an intriguing environment that people can explore freely.

Modeling

Creature

This project began with the conception of a creature I came up with while traveling, a hybrid featuring a long, snake-like body, a crocodile-like head, and imposing fangs. The 3D modeling process began with sculpting and was followed by a manual retopology of the creature.

Environment

The creature is imprisoned within the facility, bound by chains and anchored to the towering room's walls. The room also features a spiraling staircase along its walls.

Unity

Textures

Texturing was done using Substance Painter, utilizing a range of materials that were manually edited to achieve the desired appearance. The facility where the creature is kept is abandoned, and I wanted to showcase the wear and tear of the deserted structure through the use of textures.

Lighting

I aimed to highlight the massive size of both the creature and the room by utilizing lighting. The lights at the top are brighter and gradually become less visible as they descend, until the bottom of the creature and the room are completely in the dark.
