Devine Nexus

Project Type:

Personal/University, 3D environment showcase

Project Duration:

1 month

PGame Engine:

Unity

Software used:

Maya, Substance Painter, Audacity

Gameplay

Modeling

Creature

This project began with the conception of a creature I came up with while on a trip, a hybrid featuring a long, snake-like body, a crocodile-like head, and imposing fangs. The 3D modeling process started with sculpting and was followed by a manual retopology of the creature.

Environment

The creature is imprisoned within the facility, bound by chains and anchored to the towering room's walls. Additionally, the room features a spiraling staircase along its walls.

Unity

Textures

Texturing was done using Substance Painter utilizing a range of materials that I manually edited to achieve the desired appearance. The facility housing the creature is abandoned and I wanted to show the wear and tear of the deserted structure through the textures.

Lighting

I aimed to emphasize the immense size of the creature and the room through lighting. The upper lights are brighter, gradually becoming less visible as they descend, eventually becoming so dim that the bottom of the creature and the room are completely in the dark.
