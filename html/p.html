Introduction

Purification is a 2D arcade shooter set in space, were the player needs to fend off endless waves of enemy space ships and get upgrades to increase their power.

This is my first 2D game project where I explored making more complex game mechanics and sound effects from scratch.

Mechanics

Enemy Waves

The player confronts endless waves of enemy spacecrafts that must be dealt with. These waves intensify over time, with enemies growing swifter and more resilient to damage as they advance.

Upgrade System

After every 2 waves defeated, the player is presented with a selection of three random upgrades drawn from a pool of eight. The player may then choose one of these upgrades.

Score and Stats

Upon defeating enemies, the player is awarded with score points. Additionally, the player can view all of their stats by pressing the tab key.

Difficulty Selection

In the main menu, the player has the option to select the game's difficulty. Adjusting the difficulty level alters the speed and health of the enemies, as well as the score points awarded for defeating them.

Sound Effects

Making the Sounds

I aimed to create all the sounds for this project from scratch. I utilized recordings of my voice and generated sounds from sine waves. These sounds were then edited with various effects and layered to achieve the desired auditory representation of the in-game events.
